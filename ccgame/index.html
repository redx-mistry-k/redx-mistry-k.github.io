How feedback works

When you guess, you'll see two numbers: how many digits from your guess appear anywhere in the secret code (digits correct), and how many of those digits are also in the right position (positions correct).

  • Example: Secret is 4562, you guess 4251 → 3 digits correct (4,5,2) and 1 position correct (4 in first place).
  • Guess 2654 → 4 digits correct but 0 positions correct.
